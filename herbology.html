herbology

Herbology is the study and use of plants for medicinal, therapeutic, and practical purposes. Rooted in ancient traditions across cultures, it explores the properties, cultivation, and preparation of herbs to promote healing and well-being. Herbalists work with roots, leaves, flowers, and seeds to create remedies that support the body's natural processes, often using teas, tinctures, salves, or poultices. While modern medicine has drawn heavily from herbal knowledge, herbology emphasizes a holistic approach—considering not only the physical but also emotional and environmental aspects of health. Today, it continues to bridge traditional wisdom with modern science, gaining renewed interest for its natural and sustainable methods.?
